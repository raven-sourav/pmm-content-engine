## The Value Equation

**Value = (Dream Outcome x Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay x Effort & Sacrifice)**

- **Dream Outcome** — The emotional experience + tangible result. Sell the vacation, not the plane ticket.
- **Perceived Likelihood** — How confident THEY are this will work for THEM. Increased by: social proof, specificity, guarantees.
- **Time Delay** — How long before the FIRST result (not final). Faster first win = higher value.
- **Effort & Sacrifice** — What the customer gives up. Done-for-you > done-with-you > DIY.

**Key insight:** Beginners focus on the top (bigger promises). The real advantage is in the bottom — reducing time and effort toward zero makes value approach infinity.

## Grand Slam Offer Structure

1. **Pick a Starving Crowd** — Must have: massive pain, purchasing power, easy to target, growing market.
2. **Charge Premium** — Higher prices → more committed clients → better results → more profit → less competition.
3. **Create the Value Stack** — List all problems (before, during, after purchase). Transform each into a named solution. Stack as components + bonuses.
4. **Add Guarantees** — Unconditional, conditional, stacked, anti-guarantee, or performance-based.
5. **Add Real Scarcity & Urgency** — Limited quantity or limited time. Must be genuine.
6. **Name it with MAGIC** — see below.

## MAGIC Naming Formula

- **M** = Magnet word (dream outcome: "Instant," "Rapid," "Effortless," "Ultimate")
- **A** = Avatar (who it's for: "PMMs," "SaaS Founders," "B2B Marketers")
- **G** = Goal (what they achieve: "Pipeline Growth," "Category Leadership," "Win-Rate Lift")
- **I** = Interval (time frame: "30-Day," "8-Week," "Weekend")
- **C** = Container (what it is: "Blueprint," "System," "Accelerator," "Playbook")

Example: "The 8-Week Category Creation Accelerator for B2B PMMs"

## More, Better, New (Growth Hierarchy)

When optimizing content or distribution:
1. **MORE** — Do more of what's already working. Push volume.
2. **BETTER** — Optimize the constraint. Test, improve conversion.
3. **NEW** — Only after exhausting More and Better, try new channels.

Most people skip to NEW. This is wrong.
