# Content Strategy

## Key Debates
- AI content flooding vs premium on human authenticity
- Writing Style Cards as AI context vs voice loss to homogenization
- Content specialists vs one generalist + AI agents

## Common Mistakes
- Using AI without encoding personal voice first
- Not re-testing AI capabilities every 2 months as models improve
- Building on prompts instead of persistent skills

## Practitioner Wisdom
Personality-led growth is MORE important with AI. Encode voice via Writing Style Card (sentence length, punctuation, vocabulary from 5-10 best posts). Four content types: Educational (bookmark), Spicy Take (comments), Data Nugget (screenshot/share), Story Spark (reshare).
