# Positioning And Messaging

## Key Debates
- Category creation vs existing categories
- Brand-first vs product-first
- Big narrative vs niche
- Templates vs original thinking

## Common Mistakes
- Stopping at clarity (not reaching compelling+unique)
- Binary feature tables instead of execution-delta battle cards
- Positioning at wrong problem-solving level
- Copying competitors' pricing/positioning

## Practitioner Wisdom
Positioning is the most important and most skipped decision. Choose one of four product types first. Use frameworks as springboards not Mad Libs. The market positions you whether you like it or not.
