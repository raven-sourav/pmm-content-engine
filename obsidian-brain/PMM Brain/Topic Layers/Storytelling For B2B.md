# Storytelling For B2B

## Key Debates
- Data-driven vs narrative-driven content
- Authenticity vs polish
- Short-form vs long-form

## Common Mistakes
- Activity without conflict (90% of drafts)
- Flat narrative lines (no emotional oscillation)
- Feature lists instead of causation chains
- Abstract transformation without evidence/mirror scenes

## Practitioner Wisdom
All stories follow 8 emotional shapes — the line is never flat. Narrative debt keeps audiences engaged — always be owing. Build causation chains: 'Because X, you must do Y' instead of feature lists. Audit every case study: does it have genuine stakes, or just activity? Use mirror scenes (before/after) to make transformation visible.
