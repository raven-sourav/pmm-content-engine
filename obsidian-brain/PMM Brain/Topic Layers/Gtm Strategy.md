# Gtm Strategy

## Key Debates
- PLG vs sales-led vs hybrid
- Inbound vs outbound vs community
- Platform vs point solution
- Land-and-expand vs enterprise-first
- Signal-based outbound vs volume outbound
- Composable MarTech stack vs bundled platforms
- GTM engineers as new role vs marketing ops evolution

## Common Mistakes
- Hiring specialists before generalists
- SEO-first strategy when 72% discover through peer networks
- Optimizing for sales calls when 84% research before contact
- Not building expansion levers into pricing
- Treating ABM as a campaign instead of a composable system
- Bundled ABM platforms at $32K+ when composable costs 60-70% less
- Relying on website visitor deanonymization (only 42% accurate)

## Practitioner Wisdom
Modern GTM combines PLG, sales-assist, and scaled outbound. The buying committee has 5-12 people evaluating on parallel tracks. Self-service evaluation must be as polished as sales demos. Dark social > traditional demand gen for discovery.
