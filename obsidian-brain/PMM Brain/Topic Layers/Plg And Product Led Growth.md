# Plg And Product Led Growth

## Key Debates
- Pure PLG vs hybrid PLG + sales-assist
- Free tier vs reverse trial vs usage-based for PLG entry
- When to add sales to a PLG motion
- Open-core vs freemium for developer tools

## Common Mistakes
- Thinking PLG means no sales — the PLG Iceberg shows 60-80% enterprise revenue underneath
- Optimizing for signup volume over activation and expansion
- Not building usage-based sales triggers into the product
- Copying PLG playbooks without the distribution mechanics that make them work

## Practitioner Wisdom
PLG is a distribution model, not a pricing model. The best PLG companies (Figma, Miro, Zapier) build products where usage itself creates acquisition. Negative CAC and sidecar products are the most underrated PLG mechanics. Track individual-to-team expansion, not just signups.
