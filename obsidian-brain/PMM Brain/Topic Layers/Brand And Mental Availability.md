# Brand And Mental Availability

## Key Debates
- Brand vs performance marketing
- Consistency vs creativity
- Physical vs digital brand-building

## Common Mistakes
- Measuring reach/impressions instead of recall
- More content without more memory (9x noise)
- Pursuing remarkable over remembered
- Not measuring if buyers actually remember you (52% don't)

## Practitioner Wisdom
Being remarkable loses to being remembered. 92% of buyers only purchase from day-1 shortlist. Boring consistency beats clever campaigns. Pick ONE attribute and hammer it. The bar for differentiation is absurdly low — 94% of competitors are indistinguishable. It's a leadership problem, not a budget problem.
