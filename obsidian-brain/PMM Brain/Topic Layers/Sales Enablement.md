# Sales Enablement

## Key Debates
- Product training vs deal coaching
- Central PMM materials vs rep-personalized
- Enablement frequency vs depth

## Common Mistakes
- Creating materials reps don't use because they fail the 3-question filter
- Introducing net-new narratives instead of leveraging existing motions
- More enablement sessions instead of making first deals easier
- Building comprehensive decks instead of conversation-ready tools

## Practitioner Wisdom
PMM's first customer is the sales team. One closed deal generates more internal adoption than 50 enablement sessions. Position around existing sales motions. Attach to current deal cycles. Diagnostic: if only 2-3 reps 'get it,' you have an adoption problem, not an enablement problem.
