# Pricing Strategy

## Key Debates
- Usage-based vs seat-based vs hybrid
- AI credits vs bundling
- Price for access vs price for outcomes

## Common Mistakes
- Copying dominant players' pricing
- Not having pre-launch pricing conversations
- Flat fee without expansion levers (caps at ~$5M)
- Assuming AI token costs approach zero (they're rising with agentic use)

## Practitioner Wisdom
There's no perfect pricing model — every model has tradeoffs. Your job is understanding the tradeoffs. Hybrid pricing is the dominant model. Credits help manage AI economics but 2026 may swing back toward simplicity. Pricing tells a story about who you are. Engineering pricing structure (base + overages? spend caps? volume discounts?) matters more than the metric.
