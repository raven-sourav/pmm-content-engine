# Competitive Intelligence

## Key Debates
- Ignore competitors vs systematic tracking
- Feature parity vs execution differences
- Public intelligence vs dark intelligence

## Common Mistakes
- Binary feature comparison tables
- Overestimating research time
- Not quantifying intelligence output value
- Focusing on product gaps instead of execution deltas

## Practitioner Wisdom
The strongest competitive moves exploit structural facts about category leaders that create buyer disadvantages. 'Ignore competition' orthodoxy is wrong for B2B SaaS — prospects ask for comparisons in 2 of 5 high-value deals. Battle cards should guide reps to steer conversations toward your strengths, not compare checkboxes.
