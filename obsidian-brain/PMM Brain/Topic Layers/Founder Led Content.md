# Founder Led Content

## Key Debates
- Founder-written vs delegated to ghostwriters — Clark argues delegation via Content Interview scales better
- Founder-led content as primary GTM channel vs supplement?
- Building in public: real strategy or transparency theater?
- LinkedIn organic declining — dying platform or quality filter?

## Common Mistakes
- Content dilution — too many topics instead of 2-3 pillars
- Optimizing for likes instead of saves and pipeline
- LinkedIn profile as resume instead of conversion funnel
- Starting with company page instead of founder brand
- Expecting results in 30 days instead of 90-day commitment
- Using AI without decontamination — readers smell AI content

## Practitioner Wisdom
Consistency over 90 days beats any single post's quality. Audience sees 5-10% of posts — repetition is new to them. Savable content (frameworks, calendars) gets Slack-forwarded to decision-makers. Specificity is a dial — more specific = more distribution. Content Interview (30-min extraction) scales to 5x posts/week without founder writing.
