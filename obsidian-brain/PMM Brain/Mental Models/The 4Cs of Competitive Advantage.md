# The 4Cs of Competitive Advantage

## Description
True competitive advantage comes from four sources: Capability (product features), Credibility (trust signals), Convenience (ease of buying/using), and Cost (value relative to price). Most companies only look at Capability.

## How to Apply
Evaluate all four Cs. The most defensible advantages are often in Credibility or Convenience. Find the C where competitors structurally can't follow.

## When to Use
When differentiating products, building competitive narratives, or preparing sales messaging.
