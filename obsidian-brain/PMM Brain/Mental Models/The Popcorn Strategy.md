# The Popcorn Strategy

## Description
Subsidize one product to capture margins on another. Roku: hardware at cost → platform/ad revenue. The loss-leader must gateway to the high-margin business.

## How to Apply
Identify which product part could be subsidized to accelerate high-margin adoption.

## When to Use
Two-sided business models. When cost blocks adoption of your platform.
