# Stacked Opposites Strategy

## Description
Systematically audit every dimension of the incumbent experience and do the opposite. June vs Amplitude: free vs expensive, simple vs complex, auto-tracking vs manual, product-led vs sales-led. Each opposite alone is copyable. Stacked together, they create product DNA incumbents structurally can't replicate without cannibalizing existing business.

## How to Apply
List 8-10 dimensions of incumbent experience (pricing, onboarding, support, feature scope, target user, sales model). For each, define the opposite. Stack as many as you can sustain.

## When to Use
When entering a market with dominant incumbents. When single-dimension differentiation is weak.
