# 4 Tenets of Story Structure

## Description
All effective stories rest on four principles: (1) Conflict — not activity; must have genuine stakes. (2) Causation — events connect as dominos: 'Because X, you must do Y.' (3) Progress — audiences need visible advancement markers. (4) Transformation — must leave evidence; use 'mirror scenes' early and late to demonstrate change.

## How to Apply
Audit content for activity vs. conflict (90% of drafts have activity without stakes). Build causation chains instead of feature lists. Give audiences progress markers.

## When to Use
When writing case studies, story-led posts, sales narratives.
