# Content Dilution Trap

## Description
Too many topics kills distribution — algorithm can't classify you, followers can't predict value. Fix: 2-3 pillars max. Your audience sees 5-10% of posts — repetition isn't boring to them.

## How to Apply
List topics from last 30 days. If >3 themes, you're diluted. Cut and rotate angles within pillars.

## When to Use
When reach declines despite volume. When nothing compounds.
