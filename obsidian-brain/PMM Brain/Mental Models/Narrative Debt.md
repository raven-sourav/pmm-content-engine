# Narrative Debt

## Description
Every desire, investment, or fear you create in the audience is a 'debt' — an obligation to pay off. Narrative debt is what keeps audiences engaged. When all debts are paid and no new ones created, audiences disengage. The storyteller's job is to always be owing. Two opening strategies: Costly Signaling (demonstrate personal investment) and Mystery Mechanism (raise small questions that accumulate).

## How to Apply
In every post, create a debt in the first 2 lines that isn't paid off until the end. Layer new smaller debts throughout. Never let all debts resolve simultaneously.

## When to Use
When writing hooks, structuring long-form content, building engagement in any narrative.
