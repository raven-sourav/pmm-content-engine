# Three-Layer Model of PMM Influence

## Description
Influence on product roadmap operates at three layers: Blue Layer = Signal (raw market intelligence, the 'what'). Yellow Layer = Context (connecting observations, quantifying impact, the 'so what'). Green Layer = Trust (credibility that makes people listen, the 'why you'). Most PMMs operate only at Signal. Influential PMMs execute all three simultaneously.

## How to Apply
For every piece of market feedback, add context layer: attach dollar values, ARR figures, segment data. Build trust through consistent delivery before attempting to shape roadmap.

## When to Use
When struggling with roadmap influence, when PM relationship needs strengthening.
