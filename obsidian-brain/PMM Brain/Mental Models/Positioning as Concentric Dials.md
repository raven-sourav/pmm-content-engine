# Positioning as Concentric Dials

## Description
Positioning is not a single decision but a series of nested dials: Market > Category > Segment > Vertical > Buyer > Unique Value. Each dial narrows the focus. The logic must connect end-to-end.

## How to Apply
Start from the outermost dial (market definition) and work inward. At each level, ensure the logic connects to the next. If the chain breaks, the positioning is flawed.

## When to Use
When positioning feels fuzzy or when stakeholders disagree.
