# The Gen Marketer

## Description
AI will not create more specialists — it will create more generalists. The future is the pi-shaped (π) marketer: depth in 2+ sub-functions, breadth across all three (PMM, Growth, Content/Brand). Specialization was a temporary artifact of technology. AI elevates marketing jobs — execution moves to AI, humans do strategy and orchestration.

## How to Apply
Expand beyond your specialty. Run one campaign end-to-end. Rebuild org structures around Gen Marketers, not sub-functions. This is NOT 'do more with less' — it's different work.

## When to Use
When hiring marketers, when restructuring teams, when evaluating AI's impact on roles.
