# AEO (AI Engine Optimization)

## Description
SEO is dying as AI Overviews replace blue links. Organic CTR drops 70% when AI Overviews appear (2.94% → 0.84%). AEO optimizes for visibility in LLM-generated answers rather than Google rankings. Strategy shifts from keyword optimization to being the source LLMs cite — structured data, authoritative content, brand mentions in training data.

## How to Apply
Audit brand visibility in ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity for core purchase-intent queries. Build content designed to be cited by LLMs. Monitor AI search visibility alongside SEO.

## When to Use
When organic traffic declines despite stable rankings. When planning growth strategy.
