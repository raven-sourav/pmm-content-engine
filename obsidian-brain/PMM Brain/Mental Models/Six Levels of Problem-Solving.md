# Six Levels of Problem-Solving

## Description
Products solve at different levels: 1) Do a Task, 2) Fix a Workflow, 3) Improve a Process, 4) Enable a Department, 5) Fix Cross-Functional Coordination, 6) Change the Business. Higher levels command higher prices.

## How to Apply
Identify your level. If positioned at Level 1-2 but solving Level 4-5, you're underselling. If at Level 6 but delivering Level 2, you're overpromising.

## When to Use
When determining pricing strategy, value proposition, or sales narrative elevation.
