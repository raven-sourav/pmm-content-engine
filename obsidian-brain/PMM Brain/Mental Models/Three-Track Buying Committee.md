# Three-Track Buying Committee

## Description
B2B software purchasing is a three-track parallel evaluation: Financial track (ROI/cost/risk), Technical track (integration/security/capability), Marketing track (strategic alignment/adoption). By the time sales knows about an evaluation, buyers have completed 78-88% of research. Discovery starts in private peer networks (72%), not Google (51%).

## How to Apply
Design three parallel content tracks for CFO, CTO, and CMO concerns simultaneously. Make self-service evaluation as polished as sales demos.

## When to Use
When designing sales materials, when understanding why deals stall.
