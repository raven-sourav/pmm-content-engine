# The New PMM Trio: PM + PMM + Growth

## Description
The traditional PM-Engineer-Designer trio is insufficient. AI commoditizes building; the bottleneck shifts to positioning, messaging, and distribution. PM owns 'what' and 'how'; PMM owns 'who' and 'why'; Growth Owner owns acceleration and scalability. Gartner: 50% of tech CMOs say lack of collaboration is top barrier to expansion.

## How to Apply
Build PM-PMM foundation first. Use the Unified Charter (6 alignment points: Vision, Hierarchy of Pain, Magic Moment, Metrics, Playing Field, Adoption Barriers). Insert growth strategically after PMF.

## When to Use
When PMM feels siloed, when growth is stalling despite good product.
