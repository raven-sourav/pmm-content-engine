# Homepage Messaging Zones

## Description
Homepages as zones, each answering a different question. Zone 1 (Positioning: what/who/why), Zone 2 (Credibility), Zone 3 (Problem), Zone 4 (Capabilities), Zone 5 (Impact), Zone 6 (Business Case).

## How to Apply
Map your homepage against the zones. Identify which are missing, weak, or out of order.

## When to Use
When building or auditing a homepage.
