# David vs. Goliath Competitive Strategy

## Description
Find a structural fact about the category leader's business model that creates buyer disadvantages. Best advantages are ones competitors CAN'T copy because doing so would break their business.

## How to Apply
Identify one structural fact about your biggest competitor. Make that the centerpiece of your competitive narrative.

## When to Use
When competing against established category leaders.
