# The PLG Iceberg

## Description
Visible self-serve layer hides massive product-led sales motion underneath. Companies like Miro and Figma look PLG but 60-80% of revenue comes from enterprise contracts initiated by bottom-up usage. The iceberg model: free users create usage data → usage signals trigger sales outreach → sales closes enterprise deals seeded by individual adoption.

## How to Apply
Track individual-to-team expansion signals. Build sales triggers on usage patterns, not MQLs. Let product usage do the qualifying.

## When to Use
When designing PLG-to-enterprise motion. When justifying sales investment in a PLG company.
