# Negative CAC Distribution

## Description
Structure your product so users acquire other users at zero or negative cost — the product IS the distribution. Zapier's integrations appear in partner directories. Figma's multiplayer requires inviting colleagues. Miro's shared boards pull in external collaborators. The product usage itself creates acquisition.

## How to Apply
Identify which product actions naturally involve or expose non-users. Design flows that require or encourage sharing. Measure viral coefficient by feature.

## When to Use
When CAC is rising. When looking for non-paid acquisition channels.
