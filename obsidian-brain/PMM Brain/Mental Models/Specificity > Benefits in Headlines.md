# Specificity > Benefits in Headlines

## Description
Leading with generic benefits has become noise. Cognism proved this with a 40% conversion lift switching from 'world's best global sales intelligence platform' to 'We give you mobile phone numbers and B2B emails of people you want to do business with.'

## How to Apply
Replace any generic benefit with a specific, concrete description. Test: could a competitor use the exact same headline? If yes, it's too generic.

## When to Use
When reviewing homepage headlines, ad copy, or any primary messaging.
