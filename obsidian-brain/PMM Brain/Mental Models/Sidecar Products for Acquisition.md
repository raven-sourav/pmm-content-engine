# Sidecar Products for Acquisition

## Description
Build small, free tools adjacent to your core product that drive acquisition. Zapier's integration directory pages rank for every 'X + Y integration' search. HubSpot's free CRM. Zillow's Zestimate. The sidecar solves a smaller problem that leads to the bigger one.

## How to Apply
Find the search query or problem your buyers encounter BEFORE they need your product. Build a free tool for that. Make the upgrade path natural.

## When to Use
When organic acquisition is stalling. When you need a top-of-funnel wedge.
