# Four-Layer Signal System

## Description
Replace static lead scores with four stacked signal layers: (1) Contextual Fit — firmographics, tech stack. (2) Behavioral Momentum — frequency + depth patterns (pricing page 3x in 48hrs > 5 ebook downloads over 6 months). (3) Intent & Timing Triggers — funding rounds, 30%+ team growth, new VP hires creating 90-day buying windows. (4) Revenue Potential — (Target Team Size x Monthly Price x 12) with growth multipliers.

## How to Apply
Build a prioritization matrix: Champions (high on all 4), Hot Timing (high fit + active trigger), Nurture (high fit, awaiting trigger). Score frequency + depth over volume.

## When to Use
When building lead scoring. When designing outbound targeting. When arguing against legacy MQL-based approaches.
