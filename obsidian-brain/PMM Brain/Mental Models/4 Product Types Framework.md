# 4 Product Types Framework

## Description
The most important and most skipped positioning decision. Four types: (1) New Way — positioning against status quo, audience is problem-aware but solution-unaware, hardest type. (2) Vertical Solution — narrow product for specific industry/function, often most effective. (3) Buy vs. Build — replace internal/homegrown solutions. (4) 10x Better — superior product in existing category, must narrow to where you genuinely excel. If you can't fit one type, you either lack differentiation or your audience is too broad.

## How to Apply
Choose ONE product type and its comparator before writing any messaging. This determines your entire positioning approach.

## When to Use
At the start of any positioning exercise. The most critical upstream decision.
