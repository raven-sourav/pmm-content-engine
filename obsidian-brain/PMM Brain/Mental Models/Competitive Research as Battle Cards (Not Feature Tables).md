# Competitive Research as Battle Cards (Not Feature Tables)

## Description
Binary feature-comparison battle cards are ineffective. Two products can both 'have' a feature but differ massively in scope, quality, and UX. Structure battle cards around 'contrasting attributes' (execution differences). Explain where competitors excel and guide sales to steer toward your strengths. In 2 of every 5 high-value deals, prospects ask for competitor comparisons.

## How to Apply
Start with 3-5 direct competitors with highest deal-loss correlation. Build eight intelligence domains: org profile, product capabilities, search/ads, website, support, reputation, content strategy, social presence.

## When to Use
When building competitive intelligence programs, when win rates are declining.
