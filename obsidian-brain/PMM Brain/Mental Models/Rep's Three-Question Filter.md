# Rep's Three-Question Filter

## Description
Before adopting a product to sell, reps ask: (1) Is this easy to explain in a call? (2) Can I attach it to deals I'm already working? (3) Does it help me hit my number this quarter? If any answer is no, reps won't sell it regardless of enablement.

## How to Apply
Position around existing sales motions. Attach to current deal cycles. Make first deals stupidly easy. One closed deal generates more internal adoption than fifty enablement sessions.

## When to Use
When sales adoption of new product is low, when planning sales enablement.
