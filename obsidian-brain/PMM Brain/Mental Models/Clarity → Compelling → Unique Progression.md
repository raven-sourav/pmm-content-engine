# Clarity → Compelling → Unique Progression

## Description
Clear messaging is necessary but not sufficient. Most B2B companies stop at clarity. First be Clear (understand what you do), then Compelling (care enough to explore), then Unique (remember you specifically).

## How to Apply
Test messaging against three gates: Can someone explain what we do? Would they care? Would they remember us vs. competitors?

## When to Use
When reviewing homepage copy, pitch decks, or any customer-facing messaging.
