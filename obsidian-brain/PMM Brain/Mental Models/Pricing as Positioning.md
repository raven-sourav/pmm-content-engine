# Pricing as Positioning

## Description
Pricing tells a story about who you are. If your edge is lower-cost delivery, transparent pricing, or self-serve motion — make it obvious. Wrong pricing model at launch is nearly irreversible. Three drivers: (1) AI companies reach $1-10M ARR faster, wrong model = negative margins. (2) Costs are rising with agentic AI, not falling. (3) Outcome-based pricing works for verticalized solutions with clear KPIs.

## How to Apply
Have pricing conversations pre-launch: 'How solving today? What budget? Tech or headcount spend?' Build an expansion roadmap (single product + flat fee caps at ~$5M ARR). Engineer pricing structure deliberately.

## When to Use
When launching products, when revenue growth plateaus, when entering AI pricing territory.
