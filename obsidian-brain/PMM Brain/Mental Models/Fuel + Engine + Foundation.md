# Fuel + Engine + Foundation

## Description
Marketing org model: Fuel = custom content (words, design, video) aligned to audience. Engine = full-funnel growth distribution with testing/optimization. Foundation = Product Marketing research (audience, market, positioning) that informs both. Balance all three.

## How to Apply
Audit your marketing team: do you have all three? Are they balanced? Alternate hiring between fuel and engine roles.

## When to Use
When building/restructuring marketing teams, when marketing feels lopsided.
