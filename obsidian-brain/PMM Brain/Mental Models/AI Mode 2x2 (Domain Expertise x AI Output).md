# AI Mode 2x2 (Domain Expertise x AI Output)

## Description
Map employees on Domain Expertise (vertical) x AI Output volume (horizontal). Top-right = AI Mode operators (pay more). Top-left = AI Slop Machines (impressive-sounding but empty work). Bottom-right = highest-ROI AI onboarding targets. Bottom-left = at risk. Key insight: AI without domain expertise creates cleanup work. Zero times anything is still zero.

## How to Apply
Map your team to quadrants. Prioritize bottom-right for AI onboarding. Identify top-left slop by checking if expertise matches submission quality.

## When to Use
When assessing team AI readiness. When AI adoption feels productive but output quality drops.
