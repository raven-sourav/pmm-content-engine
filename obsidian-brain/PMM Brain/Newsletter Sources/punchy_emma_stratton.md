# punchy_emma_stratton

## Focus
B2B positioning and messaging for high-growth startups

## Key Frameworks
- VBF Framework: Value Proposition → Benefits → Features (hierarchy, not list)
- The 'So What?' Game: keep asking until you reach genuine buyer value
- Buzzword Swap List: replace jargon with human language
- BBQ Test: write messaging as if explaining at a barbecue

## Key Data Points


## Contrarian Insights
- Positioning is a learnable skill, not consultant magic
- Founders can and should lead their own messaging work
