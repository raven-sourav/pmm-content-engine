# Social Files (Tommy Clark)

## Focus
Founder-led LinkedIn content strategy for B2B SaaS. Runs Compound agency (150-200+ posts/week). Pattern-matching from 10,000+ posts for B2B founders.

## Key Frameworks
- 90-Day Launch Playbook — proven quarterly sequencing
- Content Ecosystem — Founder (demand) + Team (amplification) + Company page (conversion)
- Content Interview Method — 30-min extraction replacing founder writing
- Profile-as-Landing-Page — 5-zone LinkedIn profile as conversion funnel
- Content Dilution Trap — 2-3 pillar maximum
- Savable Content Framework — optimize for bookmarks and Slack-forwards

## Key Data Points
- Adam Robinson: $22-25M pipeline from founder-led LinkedIn
- Unify: $2M pipeline + 208 demos from single post
- Antimetal: 2M+ views in 24 hours
- Exit Five: ~$1.5M business via LinkedIn organic

## Contrarian Insights
- Financially irresponsible NOT to publish — content as fiduciary duty
- Content delegation > founder-written in most cases
- AI-proofing = going MORE specific and personal, not more polished
- Content burnout is a strategy problem, not a motivation problem

- LinkedIn content tactics
