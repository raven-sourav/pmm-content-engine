# Kieran Flanagan

## Focus
AI-native marketing operations, full-stack AI marketer thesis, building persistent AI systems (Skills > Prompts), AI agent adoption for GTM, SEO-to-AEO transition.

## Key Frameworks
- AI Mode 2x2 — Domain Expertise x AI Output (identify slop machines vs operators)
- Skills Over Prompts — persistent AI systems vs one-off prompts
- Context Over Instructions — better context > better prompts in 2026
- 3-Phase Content System — Setup + Ideation + Enhancement with Claude Skills
- 4 Talking Point Types — Educational, Spicy Take, Data Nugget, Story Spark
- AEO (AI Engine Optimization) — replacing SEO as primary growth channel

## Key Data Points
- Organic CTR drops 70% with AI Overviews (2.94% → 0.84%)
- LLMs simulate purchase intent with 90% accuracy
- 700-800M weekly active ChatGPT users
- Shopify: 20-40% growth while shrinking 11,600 → 8,100 headcount
- Stanford: 10-25% knowledge work performance gains from AI

## Contrarian Insights
- AI without domain expertise is the most dangerous employee — creates cleanup work
- Prompting hype was wrong about what matters — context > instructions
- Traditional marketing team structure is dead — AI creates age of the generalist
- AI makes personality-led growth MORE important, not less

## Useful For
- AI marketing strategy
- team restructuring
- content system architecture
- AEO transition
- competitive intelligence automation
