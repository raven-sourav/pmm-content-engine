# aatir_abdul_rauf

## Focus
PM-PMM collaboration, competitive intelligence, cross-functional strategy

## Key Frameworks
- New Trio: PM + PMM + Growth (replacing PM + Eng + Design)
- Unified Charter: 6 alignment points (Vision, Hierarchy of Pain, Magic Moment, Metrics, Playing Field, Adoption Barriers)
- SPACER Framework: Story, Pricing, Abilities, Competitive edge, End user, Roadmap
- Competitor Classification Matrix: Active threats, Passive threats, Indirect/substitute competitors
- Competitive Research Cheatsheet: 8 intelligence domains
- Battle Card Best Practice: Contrasting attributes (execution deltas), not binary feature tables

## Key Data Points
- Gartner: 50% of tech CMOs say lack of collaboration is top barrier to expansion
- In 2 of every 5 high-value deals, prospects ask for competitor comparisons
- Forbes: 57% identify gaining competitive advantage as top-3 priority
- Case Study — Evalufy vs AfterHire: identical resources, opposite outcomes due to positioning clarity
- Elena Verna's Growth Owner threshold: wait until $1M+ ARR, strong PMF, sufficient user data

## Contrarian Insights
- The Commoditization Paradox: AI making code cheaper makes positioning/messaging MORE valuable
- PMMs entering after sprints lock reduces their effectiveness dramatically
- 'Ignore competition' startup orthodoxy is wrong for B2B SaaS
- Public roadmaps reveal only 10% of strategy — the significant bets remain hidden
- Migration guides as PM leverage: understanding competitor products helps onboarding
