# sourav_traction_point

## Focus
Growth teardowns, customer interviews, ICP methodology, adoption strategy

## Key Frameworks
- Seven-Stage User Journey: Aha → Activated → Selected → Paid → Basic → Pro → Advocate
- Four Adoption Dimensions: Breadth, Depth, Time to Adopt, Duration
- Five-Step ICP Roadmap: Data dive → Identify best customers → Pattern spotting → Validate qualitatively → Operationalize
- Acquisition Focus When: new launches, low market share, low churn. Adoption Focus When: high churn, high CAC, subscription models

## Key Data Points
- Capsule: 24% MoM MRR growth, 100%+ monthly NRR enterprise, 120+ customers in 11 months
- Capsule: 100% RSVP rate on VIP dinner events
- Acquiring new customers costs 10x more than retaining; upselling 5-10x cheaper

## Contrarian Insights
- Fun as legitimate B2B differentiation (Capsule case study)
- VIP dinners > digital campaigns for enterprise trust-building
- Influencer strategy with marketing professionals (not traditional influencers)
