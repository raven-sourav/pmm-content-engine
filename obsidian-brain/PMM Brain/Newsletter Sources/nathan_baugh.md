# nathan_baugh

## Focus
Storytelling mechanics, narrative structure, audience engagement

## Key Frameworks
- 8 Shapes of Stories (Vonnegut): emotional arc plotted on fortune vs time axis — the line is never flat
- 4 Tenets of Story Structure: Conflict (not activity), Causation (domino chains), Progress (visible markers), Transformation (mirror scenes)
- Narrative Debt: every desire/investment/fear creates an obligation. Always be owing the audience.
- Costly Signaling vs Mystery Mechanism: two ways to open (demonstrate investment vs raise questions)
- Activity vs Conflict: characters doing things without stakes = zero tension (90% of drafts fail here)

## Key Data Points
- 6 of 8 story shapes end higher than they begin — audiences prefer net-positive transformation
- 90% of drafts have activity without genuine stakes
- Steve Jobs' 2007 iPhone presentation: masterclass in costly signaling

## Contrarian Insights
- The shape of the narrative curve matters more than the origin point — hooks are overrated vs emotional rhythm
- Sadism is generosity to the reader — protecting protagonists from difficulty is unkind to audiences
- Transformation often emerges during drafting, not planning — edit it into structure afterward
- Progress needs to be measurable — abstract 'getting better' doesn't work
