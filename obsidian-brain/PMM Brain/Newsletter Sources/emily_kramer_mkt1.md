# emily_kramer_mkt1

## Focus
B2B startup marketing strategy, team structure, AI-era playbooks

## Key Frameworks
- Fuel + Engine + Foundation: Content/Brand is fuel, Growth/Distribution is engine, PMM research is foundation
- 4 Product Types: New Way, Vertical Solution, Buy vs Build, 10x Better — the most skipped positioning decision
- Gen Marketer (π-shaped): Depth in 2+ sub-functions, breadth across all three
- K.P.O. Goals: KPIs (metrics), Projects (big bets), Ops (behind-the-scenes work that gets sacrificed without explicit goals)
- Positioning Hierarchy: Brand story → Product positioning → Messaging (tailored to segments/channels/funnel)
- Producer Role: Cross-functional dot-connector managing handoffs between fuel and engine

## Key Data Points
- Head of Marketing must be IC until team exceeds 20 people
- Single product + flat fee caps at ~$5M ARR
- AI-generated 'personalized' cold outreach is becoming ineffective due to inbox saturation

## Contrarian Insights
- Sprawling 'do-it-all' job descriptions are prescient, not misguided
- Specialization was a temporary artifact of technology, not a permanent truth
- Marketing should NOT be scoped as a pipeline/demand-gen function — that limits its impact
- Early competitors in emerging categories typically HELP you, not hurt you
- Choosing 'vertical solution' over 'new way' is almost always safer
